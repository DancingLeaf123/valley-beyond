 Studio Classroom’s 45th anniversary

   Sine 1962, StudioClassroom has encouraged many diligent students to get up early in the morning to study English. Studio Classroom started out as a radio program with a printed 2-page handout, which soon developed into a black and white twenty-page magazine. From there it expanded into a three daily radio programs with three full-color magazines and an accompanying English teaching television series. You can see that Studio Classroom has changed a lot over the years.  Doris Brougham, the founder of Studio classroom magazine and also the first English teacher on the Studio classroom radio program remembers:

   I got a tape recorder from the United States. They were new at that time and recorded a Chinese gossip program in my living room .I put the tape recorder on my bicycle and took it to the radio station for broadcast.

   It is now 45 years later and StudioClassroom is still around to help people of all ages improve their English. In order to continue to meet the need for everyday English conversation skills, StudioClassroom added another magazine to its family, called let’s talk in English, in 1981.And then in 2003 , the advanced section of StudioClassroom split off into a new magazine to meet the needs of its high level English readers. The three magazines continue to provide appropriate support to learners at all levels. But StudioClassroom didn’t stop there; it became a pioneer in its field by using different forms of mass media to provide additional learning help. In 1997 Studioclassroom put its own TV program on the air .and it became the first English teaching program broadcast on both radio and television everyday. Fast forward a few years, studio classroom is still using the latest technology; students can now use the internet ,cell phones ,ipods ,television and of course radio to enjoy the Studioclassroom program wherever they are. Through all these forms of media, learners of English from all over the world can now make use of StudioClassroom.

   As a result of its educational excellence, Studioclassroom has won numerous awards, including the Golden Tripod Award. These awards given to Studioclassroom affirmed fine quality of its production. Studioclassroom teachers, together with heavenly melody and sunshine singers visit schools to conduct English workshops. Through English activities music and drama, students learn to use English in a natural way and receive a positive influence in their lives.

   Your friend for life, Studioclassroom works hard to make a difference in your English learning and in your life.

   Join us! You can make a difference too!
