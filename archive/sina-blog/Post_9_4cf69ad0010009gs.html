A New Perspective

By: bboc 原文发布于：2007-05-10 21:43:13

      英语作文

   There was a time when I showed no interest in those reading-centered sections of our English books. They were lengthy and boring, I always complaint. And it seemed most of the class felt the same way.

   Why? The articles showcased there sometimes went to extremes just wanting us to see them go solo. With no interactions between author and his readers, all I could see in these words was a desperate attempt to force us into accepting what he himself believes. What’s ironic, it is always beyond us to follow an eloquent orator who lives in an exotic world which we can hardly have any idea of.

   I was not willing to get my mind violated this way.

   But I could not totally get rid of them. Although I pretended to take them as meaningless materials, there is no denying that they are all excellent in manipulating words and expressions. They are intruders, but at the same time, they are missionaries.

   As time passed by, I got more and more familiar with these articles. They taught me new phrases and new patterns; they helped me use them and practice, but I still found myself afraid to cross the line. A voice in my heart grew louder and louder. “Take it easy and give both of you a try” It said.

   And finally I did. Once I landed in the land where prejudice was swept away, I felt totally refreshed and free. I was too stubborn before to realize how stubborn I really was. I was locked in my little world by myself and pleased with this “wise” strategy which actually blocked me.

   Just a new perspective, now I understand, and that’s all these passages want to give to me. Take it easy to open your mind. Keep to your own mind and perceive all the other ideas--whether they irritates you or not--as a new perspective, and you’ll see what a difference it is destined to make.

   Now I read those articles every possible day. They are not only my teachers, but also my best friends. Sometimes I laugh; sometimes I fall into deep thought, but I never get lost. I know, I am now illuminating my way, with all these precious perspectives.
